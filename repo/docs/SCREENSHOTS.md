# TennisHQ — Screenshot Requirements

## Size Requirements

| Platform | Device | Resolution |
|----------|--------|------------|
| **Android** | Phone | 1080×1920 or 1242×2208 |
| **iOS** | iPhone 15 Pro Max | 1290×2796 |
| **iOS** | iPhone 8 Plus (optional) | 1242×2208 |
| **iOS** | iPad Pro 12.9" (if supporting tablet) | 2048×2732 |

## Required Screenshots (8 total)

### 1. Home — Live Matches
- **Screen:** Main tab — live match list
- **Content:** Show 4-6 live matches across different tournaments with real-time scores, set scores, and serving indicators
- **Caption:** "Real-time scores from every ATP & WTA tournament"

### 2. Player Rankings
- **Screen:** Rankings tab — ATP or WTA singles rankings
- **Content:** Top 10-15 players with rank, name, country flag, and points
- **Caption:** "Complete ATP & WTA rankings at your fingertips"

### 3. Player Profile — Overview
- **Screen:** Player detail page — overview tab
- **Content:** Player photo, key stats (career wins, titles, current rank), surface win rates chart
- **Caption:** "Deep player analytics and career stats"

### 4. Match Detail — Stats & Timeline
- **Screen:** Match detail page with stats tab active
- **Content:** Two-player stat comparison (aces, first serve %, winners, break points) with point-by-point timeline
- **Caption:** "Point-by-point match stats and timeline"

### 5. Tournament Bracket
- **Screen:** Tournament bracket view
- **Content:** Interactive bracket showing completed and upcoming rounds for a Grand Slam or Masters event
- **Caption:** "Interactive tournament brackets"

### 6. Head-to-Head (H2H)
- **Screen:** H2H comparison page
- **Content:** Two players facing off with overall record, surface breakdown, and recent match history
- **Caption:** "Head-to-head analysis with win probability"

### 7. Fantasy Team
- **Screen:** Fantasy team management page
- **Content:** User's selected fantasy team with player cards, points, salary cap remaining
- **Caption:** "Build your dream team in Fantasy Tennis"

### 8. Bracket Prediction
- **Screen:** Bracket prediction game
- **Content:** User making predictions for upcoming rounds with AI win probability hints
- **Caption:** "Predict tournament outcomes and compete"

## Screenshot Guidelines

1. **Use dark mode** (app default theme) for all screenshots
2. **Use realistic data** — real player names, plausible scores
3. **Status bar:** Clean — show time as 9:41 (iOS convention) or hide
4. **No personal info** in any screenshot
5. **Consistent device frame** — use the same mockup frame for all screenshots in a set
6. **Add captions** as overlay text at top or bottom of each screenshot (large, readable font)
7. **Background color:** Match app theme (#0d0d0d) or use a subtle gradient

## Tools for Screenshot Creation

- **Figma / Sketch** — Design frames and overlays
- **Screenshots Pro** — App Store screenshot generator
- **Fastlane Frameit** — Automated device framing
- **Simulator** — Capture from iOS Simulator / Android Emulator at exact required resolutions
