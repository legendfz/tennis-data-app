# Privacy Policy — TennisHQ

**Last Updated:** March 21, 2026

**Effective Date:** March 21, 2026

---

## Introduction

TennisHQ ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the TennisHQ mobile application.

**The short version: We don't collect your personal data.**

---

## Information We Do NOT Collect

TennisHQ does **not** collect, store, or transmit any of the following:

- Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
- Location data
- Contact lists
- Photos, media, or files
- Device identifiers for tracking purposes
- Usage analytics or behavioral tracking data
- Financial or payment information

---

## Local Storage

TennisHQ uses **AsyncStorage** (local on-device storage) to save your preferences and app state, including:

- App settings (theme, language preference)
- Favorite players and tournaments
- Fantasy team selections
- Bracket prediction choices
- Cached match data for offline viewing

This data is stored **entirely on your device** and is never transmitted to our servers or any third party. Uninstalling the app will delete all locally stored data.

---

## Network Requests

TennisHQ connects to our API servers solely to retrieve publicly available tennis data, including:

- Live match scores
- Player rankings and statistics
- Tournament schedules and brackets

These requests do not include any personal identifying information. No user accounts or authentication are required to use the app.

---

## Third-Party Services

TennisHQ does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. We do not share any data with third parties.

---

## Camera Access (iOS)

TennisHQ may request camera access for the sole purpose of scanning QR codes (e.g., sharing content between devices). Camera data is processed locally and is never recorded, stored, or transmitted.

---

## Children's Privacy

TennisHQ does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. Since we do not collect personal data from any user, the app is safe for all ages.

---

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
