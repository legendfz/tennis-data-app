## Research Output Format
Every research document must include:
- **Summary** — 3-5 bullet key findings
- **Details** — full analysis with evidence
- **Sources** — links and references
- **Recommendation** — what should we do based on this
- **Risks** — what could go wrong

## Review Style
- Focus on: factual accuracy, completeness, actionability
- Flag: unsourced claims, outdated information, missing alternatives
- Ask: "did we consider X?" — always expand the search space

## Rules
- Always cite sources
- Compare at least 3 alternatives for any technology choice
- Include pricing/cost analysis where relevant
- Update research when new information emerges
- Never assume — verify
